About us

We are based in Ormskirk and offer exciting and stimulating walks all over the beautiful Lancashire and Merseyside countryside. We guarantee your dog will come home tired and content.

We recognise every dog's needs are different and how busy daily life can be. With services starting from only £10 we ensure you the best possible service to suit the needs of both you and your pooch. We are also fully insured for everybody's peace of mind — for more information please contact us or email info@doggiewalks.co.uk.
